ExpressVPN is our top VPN recommendation. Even though the VPN market is very competitive, it has managed to stand out from the rest. It is easy to use, has a vast, fast, and secure network, unblocks Netflix, supports torrenting, and much more.

With over 3000 servers spread across 160 locations in 94 countries, you will be able to access content and services from anywhere. Its Smart Location Feature will connect you to the most optimal server with a simple click of a button.

The VPN has impressive unblocking capabilities and bypasses geo-restrictions of popular streaming services. Since the servers are super-fast, you will stream content in ultra HD on Netflix, BBC iPlayer, Hulu, and more. It is also a reliable option to unblock popular sites often blocked in workplaces and schools such as YouTube, Facebook, and Google.

Can ExpressVPN handle resource-heavy games like the League of legends? We tested it, and our gameplay was extraordinary. The VPN offers reliable and consistent speeds, so you will be able to keep up with your teammates.

The company uses advanced security and privacy features to keep you safe when browsing, streaming, gaming, or torrenting. You will get AES 256-bit encryption combined with a 4096-bit RSA key, AES 256-bit cipher, and SHA-512 HMAC authentication. There is also perfect forward secrecy that assigns you new encryption for every session.

ExpressVPN supports P2P sharing across its entire server network. Because it offers unlimited bandwidth and data, you can torrent as much as you want without any restrictions. Fortunately, it works well with uTorrent, BitTorrent, and other P2P clients.
